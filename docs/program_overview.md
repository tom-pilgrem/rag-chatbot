# Program Overview

Get the best start to your career in Data with MIP's Analytics Career Accelerator program! This 2 year immersive program gives you the perfect blend of hands-on training, mentorship and real world experience to get started in the industry.

Our expertly crafted program has introduced over 100 people to the data industry across the last 7 years. We've found that a combination of classroom based learning and hands-on experience working on real projects is the best way to get started.

Since 2018, MIP has run a program known as [The Data School Down Under](https://www.thedataschool.com.au/school/). In 2025, we rebranded this as "MIP's Analytics Career Accelerator". We're still the same team and same people!

## How It Works

### Technical Training
Immerse yourself in 8 weeks of intensive training using market-leading technologies – Power BI, Microsoft Fabric, SQL, Tableau and Alteryx. You'll have opportunities throughout the program to learn more skills and gain industry-recognised certifications.

### Projects
Weekly projects and presentations to both internal and external stakeholders help you perfect your communication and project management skills.

### Real Experience
You'll be placed on engagements with Australia's leading organisations to give you real-world experience to kick-start your new career. You'll start off as part of a wider team working alongside our senior consultants, so that you can learn what it takes to be an excellent data consultant.

## Applying to MIP's Analytics Career Accelerator Program

We do things differently and we're proud of it. Forget about the application process you might be used to — we don't want to see your CV, we want to see your passion for data.

### Step 1: Join Tableau or Power BI
Download [Tableau Public](https://www.tableau.com/products/public) or [Power BI Desktop](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/power-platform/products/power-bi/desktop) — both are free tools with loads of online learning materials. Choose the tool you've had more experience with. If you're starting fresh, we recommend Tableau.

### Step 2: Get Inspired
Look at brilliant examples online for inspiration. Check out [our blog](https://www.thedataschool.com.au/what-it-took-to-get-into-the-data-school/) to see what previous cohorts have created, and the [Tableau Public Gallery](https://public.tableau.com/app/discover/viz-of-the-day).

### Step 3: Find Your Data
Choose a dataset you're passionate about — from politics to sport, TV and popular culture. Good sources include [Data is Plural](https://www.data-is-plural.com/), [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets), [Data.world](https://data.world/), [data.gov.au](https://data.gov.au/), or the [Tableau resources page](https://public.tableau.com/en-us/s/resources). Use a real dataset — we don't accept visualisations based on fictitious or generated data.

### Step 4: Create Your Viz
Once you have your dataset, begin creating your viz! If using Tableau, publish it to Tableau Public. Need help? Get in touch with any of the team or coaches.

### Step 5: Share It With Us
Share your Tableau Public URL via our application form. If using Power BI, upload the file as an additional document or send it as an email attachment.

### Step 6: Iterate
We'll provide feedback and suggestions on how to improve your submission. You'll then share a second version — this improved version is what we care about most. Don't worry about how the first one looks, just send it!

### Step 7: The Interview
If we see passion, creativity and a flair for data, we'll follow up personally. First, a video call where you present your visualisation for 10 minutes. The best candidates will then be invited for a final interview with the rest of the team.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can MIP provide sponsorship for individuals without working visas for Australia?**
Not currently. We may offer this in the future. All applicants need to have authorisation to work in Australia.

**What can I do to have the best chance at getting a place?**
Review the Tableau Public Gallery and check out featured authors to see the quality of published work. Check out The Data School blog to see what previous cohorts have created.

**Are there details about what I'll learn?**
The focus is to make you a brilliant analyst, covering a wide variety of hard and soft skills. A great amount of time will be spent on Tableau Desktop, Power BI, SQL, Fabric and Alteryx. You'll also learn how to present, tell stories, and work with clients. At the end of 2 years, you'll be an incredibly well-rounded analyst.

**What should I include in my application viz?**
Do it on a topic you're interested in — you'll have more context and be able to find meaningful insights. Make sure it's a real dataset with real values.

**When can I start?**
We have two intakes per year: one in April and one in October.

**What happens if I don't get in first time?**
We encourage you to apply again for a future cohort. You'll have learnt a lot from your first attempt and be able to apply those learnings next time.
